White-hat SEO practices are those that conform to the guidelines set forth by search engines like Google. These practices focus on creating high-quality content, building natural backlinks, and optimizing websites for user experience. Black-hat SEO practices, on the other hand, involve using deceptive or manipulative techniques to improve a website's ranking in search results. Search engine optimization (SEO) is a complex and ever-changing field, but there are some basic tips that can help you improve your website's ranking in search engine results pages (SERPs). Here are five tips from Austin SEO companies:

Tip 1: Create high-quality content.

The content on your website should be informative, engaging, and well-written. It should also be relevant to your target audience and keyword-rich. Creating high-quality content will help you attract more organic traffic to your website and improve your ranking in SERPs.

Tip 2: Build backlinks.

Backlinks are links from other websites to your website. Building backlinks is an important part of SEO because it helps to improve your website's authority and credibility. You can build backlinks by submitting your website to directories, guest posting on other websites, and participating in online forums.

Tip 3: Optimize your website for mobile devices.

More and more people are using their mobile devices to access the internet. If your website is not optimized for mobile devices, you could be losing out on potential customers. Make sure your website is easy to navigate on a mobile device and that it loads quickly.

Tip 4: Use social media.

Social media is a great way to connect with potential customers and promote your website. Share interesting content on social media, and make sure to include links to your website. You can also use social media to run ads that target specific demographics.

It is important to track your SEO results so that you can see what is working and what is not. There are a number of tools available that can help you track your website's traffic and ranking in SERPs. By tracking your results, you can make adjustments to your SEO strategy as needed.
