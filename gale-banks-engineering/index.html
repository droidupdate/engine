Turbochargers

Turbochargers are a key component of many Gale Banks Engineering engines. Turbochargers are devices that use exhaust gases to spin a turbine, which in turn drives a compressor. The compressor then forces more air into the engine, which results in increased power and efficiency.

  • Increased power

    Turbochargers can significantly increase the power output of an engine. This is because turbochargers allow more air to be forced into the engine, which results in a more powerful combustion process.

  • Improved efficiency

    Turbochargers can also improve the efficiency of an engine. This is because turbochargers allow the engine to operate at a lower RPM, which reduces fuel consumption.

  • Reduced emissions
