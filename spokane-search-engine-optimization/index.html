Link building

Link building is an essential part of Spokane search engine optimization (SEO). Backlinks are links from other websites to your website. They are one of the most important factors that search engines use to determine the authority and credibility of a website. The more high-quality backlinks a website has, the higher it will rank in search engine results pages (SERPs).

  • Relevance: The relevance of a backlink is important. A backlink from a website that is relevant to your business or industry is more valuable than a backlink from a website that is not.
  • Authority: The authority of a website is also important. A backlink from a website with a high authority is more valuable than a backlink from a website with a low authority.
  • Placement: The placement of a backlink is also important. A backlink that is placed in the body of a blog post or article is more valuable than a backlink that is placed in the sidebar or footer.
  • Anchor text: The anchor text of a backlink is also important. The anchor text is the text that is used to link to your website. It should be relevant to the content of the page that you are linking to.

By building high-quality backlinks from relevant websites, businesses can improve their website's authority and credibility. This can lead to higher rankings in SERPs, more traffic, and more sales.

Mobile optimization

Technical SEO

Technical SEO is an important part of any Spokane search engine optimization (SEO) strategy. It involves optimizing the website's technical aspects to make it more crawlable, indexable, and user-friendly. This can help improve the website's ranking in search engine results pages (SERPs), which can lead to more traffic and conversions.

One of the most important aspects of technical SEO is site speed. A slow-loading website can frustrate users and cause them to leave the site before it has fully loaded. This can lead to a decrease in traffic and conversions. Google and other search engines also use site speed as a ranking factor, so a slow website is likely to rank lower in SERPs.

Another important aspect of technical SEO is crawlability. Crawlability refers to how easily search engine bots can access and index the website's content. If a website is not crawlable, it will not be able to rank in SERPs. There are a number of factors that can affect crawlability, such as the website's structure, navigation, and use of robots.txt files.

Indexability refers to how well the website's content is indexed by search engines. If a website's content is not indexed, it will not be able to rank in SERPs. There are a number of factors that can affect indexability, such as the website's content quality, use of keywords, and use of meta tags.

Technical SEO is a complex and ever-changing field, but it is essential for any business that wants to improve its online visibility. By optimizing the website's technical aspects, businesses can improve their ranking in SERPs, increase traffic, and generate more leads and sales.
