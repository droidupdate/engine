Curriculum

The curriculum of MS Systems Engineering online programs is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the systems engineering process. This includes the ability to analyze, design, and integrate complex systems. Students will learn about the different phases of the systems engineering lifecycle, from requirements gathering and analysis to design, implementation, and testing. They will also learn about the different tools and techniques used in systems engineering, such as modeling and simulation.

  • Systems Analysis: The first phase of the systems engineering process is systems analysis. In this phase, students will learn how to identify and define the problem that the system will solve. They will also learn how to gather and analyze requirements from stakeholders.
  • Systems Design: The second phase of the systems engineering process is systems design. In this phase, students will learn how to design a system that meets the requirements identified in the analysis phase. They will also learn about the different types of systems architectures and how to select the best architecture for a given system.
  • Systems Integration: The third phase of the systems engineering process is systems integration. In this phase, students will learn how to integrate the different components of a system into a working whole. They will also learn about the different types of integration testing and how to conduct integration testing.

The curriculum of MS Systems Engineering online programs is designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills they need to be successful systems engineers.
