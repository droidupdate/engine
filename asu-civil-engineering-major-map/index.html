Only programs that meet the ABET standards are accredited.

  • Curriculum: The ABET accreditation process requires programs to have a curriculum that is up-to-date and relevant to the needs of the engineering profession. The curriculum must also provide students with a strong foundation in the fundamentals of engineering.
  • Faculty: The ABET accreditation process also requires programs to have a qualified faculty with a strong commitment to teaching. Faculty members must be actively involved in research and professional development to ensure that they are up-to-date on the latest advances in the field.
  • Facilities: The ABET accreditation process also requires programs to have adequate facilities to support student learning. These facilities include classrooms, laboratories, and computer labs.
