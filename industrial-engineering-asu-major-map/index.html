Coursework

The coursework in an industrial engineering major map at ASU provides a solid foundation in the core disciplines of mathematics, science, engineering, and business. This coursework is essential for preparing students to work as industrial engineers in a variety of industries.

  • Mathematics: Industrial engineers use mathematics to model and analyze complex systems, such as manufacturing processes and supply chains. They also use mathematics to develop optimization algorithms and other tools that can be used to improve the efficiency of organizations.
  • Science: Industrial engineers need a strong understanding of science to understand the physical world and the laws of nature. This knowledge is essential for designing and operating efficient industrial processes.

  • Business: Industrial engineers need a strong understanding of business principles in order to work effectively with other professionals in the organization. They also need to understand the financial implications of their decisions.

ABET Accreditation

ABET accreditation is a globally recognized certification that assures the quality and industry relevance of educational programs in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). For industrial engineering programs, ABET accreditation is a valuable indicator of program quality and industry alignment.

The industrial engineering ASU major map is designed to meet the ABET accreditation standards. This means that the program undergoes regular review by ABET to ensure that it meets the highest quality standards. The program is also required to demonstrate that it is responsive to the needs of industry. This ensures that graduates of the program are well-prepared for the workforce.

There are many benefits to ABET accreditation for students and employers alike. For students, ABET accreditation provides assurance that they are receiving a high-quality education that will prepare them for successful careers. For employers, ABET accreditation provides confidence that graduates of the program have the skills and knowledge necessary to be successful employees.

ABET accreditation is an important component of the industrial engineering ASU major map. It is a valuable indicator of program quality and industry relevance. Students who graduate from ABET-accredited programs are well-prepared for successful careers in industrial engineering.
