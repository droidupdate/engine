Technology

Xcentric Mold & Engineering Inc. leverages cutting-edge technology throughout its mold manufacturing processes, empowering them to deliver exceptional results that meet the demands of modern industries.

  • Advanced CAD/CAM Software: Xcentric Mold & Engineering Inc. employs sophisticated CAD/CAM software to design and simulate molds with precision and efficiency. This technology enables them to create complex mold designs, optimize part geometry, and conduct virtual mold trials, reducing the need for physical prototyping and accelerating the development process.
  • CNC Machining Centers: The company utilizes state-of-the-art CNC machining centers to manufacture molds with unmatched accuracy and repeatability. These machines are equipped with advanced cutting tools and precise motion control systems, allowing for the production of intricate mold components and high-quality surface finishes.
  • Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMMs): Xcentric Mold & Engineering Inc. employs CMMs to inspect molds and ensure dimensional accuracy. These machines use touch probes or laser scanners to measure complex geometries and compare them to digital models, providing detailed reports and identifying any deviations from specifications.
  • Mold Flow Simulation: The company utilizes mold flow simulation software to analyze and optimize the flow of molten plastic during the injection molding process. This technology helps identify potential defects, such as air traps or weld lines, and enables engineers to design molds that produce parts with consistent quality and optimal mechanical properties.

By harnessing these cutting-edge technologies, Xcentric Mold & Engineering Inc. can deliver molds with exceptional precision, efficiency, and quality, solidifying their position as a leader in the custom plastic injection molding industry.
