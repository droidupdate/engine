Variable valve timing

Variable valve timing (VVT) is a technology that allows the timing of a car's valves to be adjusted while the engine is running. This can be done to improve performance, fuel economy, and emissions.

  • Improved performance

  • Improved fuel economy

    VVT can also be used to improve fuel economy by reducing pumping losses. Pumping losses are the energy that is lost when the engine has to move air in and out of the cylinders. By optimizing the timing of the valves, VVT can reduce pumping losses and improve fuel economy.
